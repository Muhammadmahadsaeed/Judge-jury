Eco Sustainable Denim Product

JUDGE & JURY “Disturbing The Peace” is a completely sustainable denim collection derived from eco-friendly resources such as sustainably organic grown crops and/or PCW (post consumer waste) recycled materials utilizing only eco friendly washes brought to you from the deserts of Dubai, where water is precious. Sustainability is key to our ethos & culture which provides a framework for how we manage our impact to the social and economic development of the communities that we operate. As a responsible brand, we are always looking to manage our environmental footprint. Less Chemical Less Energy
